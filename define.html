Define - Transmission Line Fault Detection

In the define phase of transmission line fault detection, we establish a clear understanding of the problem and the goals of our fault detection system.

During this phase, we work on defining the scope of our project, identifying specific types of faults we aim to detect, and setting performance criteria for our detection system.

Key considerations during the define phase include:

  • Defining the objectives and goals of the fault detection system.
  • Understanding the types of transmission line faults and their characteristics.
  • Identifying relevant data sources and sensors.
  • Setting performance metrics for accuracy and reliability.

The define phase is a crucial step in the development of an effective fault detection solution. It provides a clear roadmap for the design and implementation of the system, ensuring that it meets the specific needs of the power grid and its operators.
